4 aspects of Worship:

Why do we worship Allah?

How do we worhship Allah?

How do we improve our worship?

What are the levels of worship (quantity & quality)?

Why do we worship Allah?

Because ..... Allah has made us Muslims and blessed us with so many things .... Not a fundamental reason, rather they are secondary

Fundamental reason is:

He deserves to be worshipped

How do we worhship Allah?

With knowledge

How do we improve our worship?

Through essense, but how?

First, know about Fiqh rules of an act i.e. memories of how Prophet(pbuh) used to pray

Second, know about Fadhael i.e. motivating blessings/benefits of Wudu

What are the levels of worship (quantity & quality)?

L1. Islam - Tawheed, 5 daily Fard, 30 days of Fasting, 2.5% Zakah, 1 Hajj if able

L2. Emaan - Increase in Sunnah prayers i.e. 12 sunnah

L3. Ihsaan - Worship Allah as if you see Him, else know that Allah sees you. Convert 24/7 life into worship through this consciousness
