Worship in Islam

Based on Yasir Qadhi's - Why do we worship God?"

Compiled By Shahed Molla

Aspects of Worship


  • WHY do we worship Allah?
  • HOW do we worhship Allah?
  • What can we do to IMPROVE our worship?
  • What are the LEVELS of worship?

WHY do we worship Allah?


  • Because .....
    • Allah has made us Muslims
    • And blessed us with so many things
    • But these are NOT fundamental reasons

  • Rather fundamental reason is:
    • He deserves to be worshipped

HOW do we worhship Allah?


  • With knowledge

What can we do to IMPROVE our worship?


  • By bringing essense to the acts of worship, but HOW?
    • First, know about Fiqh rules of an act i.e. how Prophet(pbuh) used to pray
    • Second, know about Fadha'el - blessings of the act

  • Then internalize through pondering and practice

What are the LEVELS of worship?


L3     Ihsaan - Worship Allah as if you see Him, else know that Allah sees you. Convert 24/7 life into worship through this consciousness

L2     Emaan - Increase acts of worship beyond Fard i.e. 12 rakah sunnah prayers

L1     Islam - Tawheed, 5 daily Fard prayers, 30 days of Fasting, 2.5% Zakah, 1 Hajj if able
